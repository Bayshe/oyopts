PTS WINS MOBILITY COMPANY OF THE YEAR AWARD.

The Pacesetter Transport Services wins the Mobility Company of the Year Award at the just concluded Ibadan Tech Expo, 24 Awards.

The award ceremony which is regarded as the biggest tech event in the Capital City of Oyo State, Ibadan was held on Saturday, 28th September, 2024 at the International Conference Centre(ICC), University of Ibadan, hosting dignitaries, tech experts and enthusiasts across the State and beyond.

The Pacesetter Transport Services was categorized amongst companies like Bolt, In-Drive, and Mevron for the Mobility Company of the Year Award.

Beyond doubts, the Pacesetter Transport Services, currently peculiar with the State, having digitalized over 500,000 commuters in the State; and being the only face of succour for the masses of the State in this current state of economic despondency in the Country; providing unmatched affordability, and yet comfortability; are well deserving of the Mobility Company of the Year Award.

The Chairman and Sole Administrator of the Pacesetter Transport Services, Hon. Dr. Salami Ibraheem Oladeji DIKKO dedicates the award to the Governor of the State, His Excellency, Engr. Oluseyi Abiodun Makinde.
