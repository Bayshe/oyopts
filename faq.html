FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT PACESETTER TRANSPORT SERVICES

  • What services does Pacesetter Transport Services provide?

    • We offer a variety of transport solutions, including interstate, intrastate, and intercity transportation, as well as flexible hired services for both individuals and businesses. Whether you need one-time transport or recurring services, we’ve got you covered.

  • What are your hired services?

    • Our hired services allow you to book a vehicle and driver for a set period, tailored to your specific needs. Whether it's moving items, supporting an event, or assisting with urgent deliveries, our hired transport service provides flexibility to meet your schedule.

      • Do you offer both personal and business transportation solutions?

    • Yes, we serve both personal and business clients. Whether you're an individual needing help with a move or a company requiring regular transportation services, we can customize our offerings to suit your needs.

  • Do Omituntun buses provide free Wi-Fi for commuters?

    • Yes, Omituntun buses offer free Wi-Fi services to enhance the commuting experience for passengers.

      • How many seats does the Omituntun bus have?

    • Omituntun buses are available in two categories: 56-seater and 46-seater, providing ample seating capacity to accommodate passengers comfortably.

  • What payment method is accepted for boarding the Omituntun bus?

    • The Omituntun bus service uses the Pacesetter Card payment system, a cashless and convenient method for passengers to pay for their rides.

      • Does the balance on my Omituntun card expire if I do not use it within a certain time?

    • No, the balance on your Omituntun card does not expire, allowing you to use it at your convenience without any time restrictions.

      • What is the minimum amount I can load onto the Omituntun card ?

    • You can top up your Omituntun card with a minimum amount of ₦200 or more.

      • Can I top up my Omituntun card from my bank account?

    • No, topping up directly from a bank account is not available. However, agents on board the Omituntun buses are available to assist you with reloading your card."

      • Why do I need to tap out with my Omituntun card when getting off the bus?

    • It is very important for all commuters to tap out for the accurate fares to be deducted and when commuters fails to tap out, the validators assumed they have gone through the maximum trip and deducts equivalent fare.
